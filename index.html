LORE

Humans and machines became two different factions. As both sides competed over natural resources, the machines started creating a destructive byproduct causing pollution. It turned the sky purple and led to some humans going crazy. One of these humans was a hacker turned botanist who was infected and created a fungus that mutated bugs into large and glowing mutants.

This further increased tensions between robots and humans leading but a lone group decided to end the outbreak. One, is a human who was infected by the fungus and gained magic powers and the other a robot controlling nano machines within her to stop the fungus from becoming dangerous and allowing it to be tinkered with to improve its abilities.
